about jehan bank

jehan is a bank that has thrived over 30 years in a rapidly evolving market and economy. Having grown from humble beginnings with visionary intent, we are the first private bank in Ethiopia to exceed a billion profit mark in the history of Ethiopian Private Banks in the financial year 2020 since inception, we have expanded through a combination of substantial organic growth and an enhanced customer service experience
